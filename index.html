Good to Know

Questions, answered.

Who can request a cake?+

Requests come from staff, caseworkers, or approved program contacts at the shelters and programs we serve — not directly from families — so we can coordinate safely and reliably.

How much does a cake cost the family?+

Nothing. Every cake is entirely free, always. We are funded by volunteers and donations so the celebration never comes with a price.

How far in advance should we request?+

Ideally one to two weeks ahead. That gives our bakers time to plan, shop, and decorate something special — including any allergy-friendly needs.

I want to help but I cannot bake — is that alright?+

Of course. We also need help with supplies, deliveries, outreach, and coordination. Fill out the volunteer form and we'll find the role that fits you.
